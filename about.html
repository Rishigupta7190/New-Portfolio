My name is Rishi Gupta, and I hail from the beautiful city of Gwalior, located in the heart of Madhya Pradesh, India. I spent my formative years at Kiddys Corner Hr. Sec. School, where I completed both my 10th and 12th standards.

Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelor of Technology degree with a specialization in Computer Science Engineering at VIT Vellore. My academic journey at VIT has been enriching, providing me with a solid foundation in computer science principles and cutting-edge technologies.

Beyond academics, I actively engage in extracurricular activities to broaden my horizons and develop essential skills. I am honored to have participated in Gravitas, VIT's renowned annual technical event, where I gained valuable insights and experiences in the realm of technology and innovation. Additionally, I have contributed my time and efforts as a core member of the Institution of Engineers (IEI) chapter, where I had the opportunity to collaborate with like-minded peers and contribute to the engineering community.

Looking ahead, my aspiration is to embark on a fulfilling career as a software engineer with one of the leading multinational corporations. I am driven by a passion for leveraging technology to solve complex problems and make a positive impact on society.
